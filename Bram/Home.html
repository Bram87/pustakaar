Pustakaar is a Mobile Library program. The program currently works through schools that lack the infrastructure or means to have a library system. The students of these schools typically have no means to access books outside their standard curriculum either at school or at home. pustakaar works through a network of volunteers who visit these targeted schools and provide the students an opportunity to borrow books every week. Pustakaar volunteers also conduct a regular reading program where they read books to the students and play interactive games with the students to help develop their language skills and build their confidence.
